Data Privacy & Local Processing

Your privacy is our priority. Unlike many other AI tools, Expression Analysis AI does not upload your photos to any server. All image analysis is performed locally within your own web browser using TensorFlow.js and Google's Teachable Machine technology. Your images never leave your device.
