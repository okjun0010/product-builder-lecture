How to Use

  1. Prepare your photo: Take a clear photo of your face or choose one from your device.
  2. Upload: Click the "Upload Image" button above and select your file.
  3. Analyze: Our AI model will instantly process the image and show you the probability of each expression.

Privacy Fact: Your images are processed entirely on your device. We never see or store your photos.

About the Technology

This tool uses Google's Teachable Machine, a fast and easy way to create machine learning models. Under the hood, it's powered by TensorFlow.js, which allows high-performance AI models to run directly in your web browser.

By using your device's GPU/CPU locally, we can provide instant results while ensuring your personal data stays on your machine.
